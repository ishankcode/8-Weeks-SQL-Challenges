### Challenges

**Q1. What is the total amount each customer spent at the restaurant?**

````sql
SELECT S.customer_id, SUM(price) As "Total_spend $"
FROM sales S INNER JOIN menu M ON S.product_id = M.product_id  
GROUP BY customer_id;
````
